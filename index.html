How to Submit a Claim

Instead of spending time filling forms manually, the Finance Team is now split into Liaisons and Form Processors — so that errors are caught earlier and your claim goes through without a hitch.

💰
CCA Treasurer
STEP 1
CCA Treasurer sends claim details to their Liaison
Telegram the following to your Liaison:
Portfolio: _______________
CCA: _______________
Claim Description: _______________
Other Emails in Claims: _______________
Remarks: _______________
+ Screenshots of all Receipts & Bank Transactions
passes details to
📋 Finance Team
🔗
Liaison
STEP 2
Liaison checks everything is correct
They go back and forth with the Treasurer until all the details are confirmed. Once happy, they submit the Google Form.
form submitted →
⚙️
Form Processor
STEP 3
Form Processor processes the submission
The form response is recorded in the system. The Form Processor then does two things at the same time:
simultaneously
⚙️
Form Processor
STEP 4A
Sends an email to the CCA Treasurer
The email tells the CCA Treasurer exactly what to copy-paste and send to rh.finance@u.nus.edu (which is the Form Processor's inbox), including all attachments.
⚙️
Form Processor
STEP 4B
Generates the claim documents
The system auto-generates the LOA, Summary, and RFP forms and saves them to Google Drive.
⏳ waiting for treasurer
💰
CCA Treasurer
STEP 5
CCA Treasurer sends the email to rh.finance@u.nus.edu
Copy-paste the exact wording from the email received, attach all the files, and send to rh.finance@u.nus.edu — this goes directly to the Form Processor. Do not forward — compose a new email.
email received →
📋 Finance Team
⚙️
Form Processor
STEP 6
Form Processor adds the email screenshot & compiles everything into one PDF
All the forms are merged into a single PDF ready for submission.
final step →
🏛️
Finance Office
STEP 7
PDF is submitted to the Finance Office
The compiled PDF is handed in. Once approved, the reimbursement is processed. ✓
