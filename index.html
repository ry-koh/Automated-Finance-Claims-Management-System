How to Submit a Claim

The Finance Team is now split into Liaisons and Form Processors — so errors are caught earlier and claims go through without a hitch.

✗  Before — Manual Process
✗ CCA Treasurer writes their own email — often with errors, inconsistent formatting
✗ Limited to 5 receipts per email — multiple emails needed for one event
✗ Finance team manually parses every email and attachment to extract claim data
✗ LOA, Summary, and RFP forms all filled in manually
✗ Errors only caught at LOA stage or after submission — causing delays and requiring the entire process to restart from Step 1
✓  Now — Automated System
✓ Treasurer just Telegrams details to their Liaison — no email writing needed
✓ No receipt limit — send everything for an entire event at once
✓ Liaison catches errors early before submission — fewer back-and-forths later
✓ LOA, Summary, and RFP auto-generated from the form — zero manual form filling
✓ Email to Treasurer auto-generated with exact wording — no room for mistakes
