Overview
Your Role in the Process
Your job is just two actions: send a Telegram message with your claim details, then send a new email when you receive instructions from Finance Team. Finance Team handles everything in between.
01
Send a Telegram
Your first and main task

What each field means
Portfolio
Your CCA's portfolio group (e.g. Sports, Culture, Welfare). Skip if your Liaison already knows.
CCA
Your CCA's name. Skip if your Liaison already knows.
Claim Desc
Brief name of the event or purchase. E.g. "Disc Claim" or "Welfare RHingo Claim".
Others
Emails of others involved in the claim. Leave blank if none.
Receipts
For each receipt, send the matching bank transaction screenshot together. Don't send all files in a pile — your Liaison needs to know which bank screenshot belongs to which receipt.
No receipt limit. Previously you could only send 5 per email. Now send everything for an entire event in one Telegram message.
Step 1 — Telegram
What to Attach — Receipts & Screenshots
🧾
Receipt / Invoice
Photo or screenshot of the physical receipt or invoice from the merchant. Required for every purchase.
🏦
Bank Transaction Screenshot
A screenshot of the transaction record from your bank app or PayNow confirming payment was made. Must match the receipt it belongs to.
Missing a bank screenshot? Finance Team won't be able to process the claim. Make sure every receipt has a matching bank transaction photo.
02
Wait & Watch
Finance Team handles things from here
Step 2 — Wait
What Finance Team Does After You Send
You will receive an email from Finance Team. The subject line will be your claim's reference code (e.g. 2526-WELFARE-WELFARE COMM-001). This is your next cue.
03
Send the Email
Your second and final active task
Step 3 — Send Email
The Email You Receive & Act On
What you need to do
1
Create a NEW email
Do not reply to the Finance Team email. Open a fresh compose window.
2
Copy the To, CC, Subject
Use the exact To, CC, and Subject shown in the dotted box. Don't change the subject line.
3
Paste the body & send
Copy everything below the line in the dotted box, paste as your email body, then send.
Do not reply to the Finance Team email — you must create a brand new email addressed to the Finance Director.
Step 3 — Forward Email
Your Reference Code — Keep It Handy
Use this code when following up with Finance Team about your claim status. It will also appear as the subject of the Finance Team email you receive.
Do not change the subject line when sending to the Finance Director — keep it exactly as given. This is how the claim is tracked.
04
Wait for Reimbursement
Finance Team wraps up and you get paid
Step 4 — Wait
What Happens After You Send
📸
Screenshot added & compiled
Finance Team screenshots your sent email, adds it to your documents, and compiles everything into one PDF.
🏛️
NUS OFN approval
Finance Office submits the PDF to NUS Office of Finance (OFN). Once NUS approves, they reimburse the Finance Director.
💸
Finance Director pays you
After the Finance Director receives the reimbursement from NUS, they pay you back via PayNow.
Expect at least a month. The process goes through NUS OFN which is outside Finance Team's control. Follow up with your Liaison using your Reference Code if it's been unusually long.
